About Me

User Experience Design is a vital aspect of software development. The design is the user’s first impression of a wonderful product.

As a UX Designer I take pride in producing high quality work with real world application. My designs are centered around a holistic strategy that combines knowledge of Computer Science, Human Factors Psychology, and Business Strategy.

USER EXPERIENCE DESIGN B.S.

My education separates me from many User Experience Designers because I was able to tailor my degree to fit the specific needs of a UX Designer. By taking advantage of a unique opportunity offered at the University of Utah, I created my own degree and will soon be the first person to graduate in UX Design from my university. This degree combines design, computer science, business, and psychology. Elements which will allow me to become adverse in my skills and gain knowledge that will help me work productively on a development team.
