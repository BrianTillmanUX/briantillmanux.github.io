Prototype I

First, Rick needs to add a new vehicle. He selects “search for a machine”.

Rick cycles though the options, selects the dropdown menu, and finds his ATV.

Rick wants to fix his broken handle bar so he selects the “damage report” button.

He is directed to a tutorial that teaches him how to use the tools and find the part he needs to change.

Now that Rick knows how to navigate the environment, he finds the part for his broken handle bar and selects it.

He is now presented with a price comparison page that allows him to compare the cost of fixing it himself or having a mechanic fix it for him.

Rick chooses to fix it himself because the process has an “easy” rating and is estimated to only take an hour and a half. Now at the “fix it” menu he can see the item and any other current project he is working on.

Using the resources provided, Rick clicks on video tutorial and a 3D walkthrough.

Rick is confident and ready to buy. From watching the video tutorial, he knows he is also going to need a special tool. He adds the Suzuki Custom Triangle Allen Wrench to his purchase.

This completes Rick’s order. Once his package arrives, he will be able to go back and watch the “fix it” video while he works on his ATV.
