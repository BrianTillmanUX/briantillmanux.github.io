Maria Garcia

get over

1. [get over something] to start to feel happy or well again after something bad has happened to you.

· It can take weeks to get over an illness like that.

· Jack is pretty upset, but he’ll get over it.




2. [get something over] to do something or allow something to happen, because you want it to be finished or you want to start something else

· This was an attempt to stop reporters from getting at the truth.




3. Can’t get over something used for saying that you are very surprised by something or think it is funny

· I just can’t get over how well we played!
